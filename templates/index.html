unofficial podcast feed

Rinse FM's website CMS doesn't enclose the podcast media files in it's podcast RSS feeds, making it impossible to subscribe with podcatchers.

I was using @levelsio's Rinse FM podcast with great success for a while. Unfortunately, it seems to break whenever there's a format-change or any erroneous data-entry on Rinse FM's side. I've tweeted @levelsio in the past to fix issues, but it seems unfair to bug him to keep it maintained, and he's not open-sourced it yet.

So no worries, I built my own unofficial RSS feed! It's open-source and feeds are available below:
